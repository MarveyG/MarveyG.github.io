About Me

Design Architect

I'm Omole Tosin,
a specialized architect, studied Architecture at the Kwara State Polytechnic Nigeria, I furthered my mastering of Architectural Designs and rendering with several self tutorials and courses online. Now I design and render Architectural projects skillfully and this prowess comes with nine years of experience and I have a degree in Architecture.

I'm married and with two wonderful kids and currently live in the city of Ilorin Kwara State Nigeria. Also being the CEO of Tshine Studio Limited located also in Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria where we give you quality and standard deliveries on time.
We promise the best anyday, anywhere and anytime as we have clients from different countries of the world and deliver remote jobs, contract jobs and site projects management. A trial will definitely convince you. I'm diligent and easy to work with, my clients can testify to this, because your satisfaction as a client is my goal. I'm passionate about architecture and wouldn't trade it for nothing.

I'm proficient in the following:

  • Landscape design and rendering

  • Interior design and rendering

  • Exterior 3d design and rendering


I'm fast, reliable and communicative, I pay more attention to quality, details and functionality.
I'm always ready to offer a wide range of design and layout solutions to make clients ideas and visions become a reality. Very well experienced in residential and commercial projects. In the past, as part of a design team, I have collaborated with the design of a community centre as well as many residential projects.
If you choose me, you can be assured that you are getting top-notch expertise in your project, as well as someone who will complete your jobs correctly.
